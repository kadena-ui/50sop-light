  • Strictly necessary cookies. Required for the Site to function, including session management and security
  • Performance and analytics cookies. Help us understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it (such as Google Analytics)

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services we use, including Google Analytics, our email service provider, and our payment processor. These third parties have their own privacy policies that govern their cookie use.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or notify you when a cookie is being set. Disabling cookies may affect Site functionality. For more information on managing cookies, visit your browser's help section.
