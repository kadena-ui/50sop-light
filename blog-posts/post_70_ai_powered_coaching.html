How to build your
AI coaching model
  • Start with your existing IP — your frameworks, questions, and decision trees are the training material. The AI amplifies what you already know.
  • Choose the right platform — tools like CoachHub, BetterUp, or custom GPT builds allow coaches to deploy AI at different levels of technical complexity
  • Define the human trigger points — design the moments when AI escalates to human. Career pivots, crisis moments, and high-stakes decisions are always human.
  • Price the tiers clearly — AI access, AI plus human, and human-only should be three distinct, clearly communicated tiers with different value propositions
  • Sell access to organisations first — corporate HR and L&D budgets are 10 to 100 times larger than individual coaching budgets. Start there.
