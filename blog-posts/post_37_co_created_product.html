4
What to establish
before you build
anything together
  • IP ownership in writing — who owns what if the partnership ends? Establish this before a single slide is built.
  • Revenue split agreement — 50/50, contribution-weighted, or tiered by role — whatever it is, document it first
  • Decision rights — who has final say on pricing, launches, and pivots? Ambiguity here ends partnerships.
  • Exit terms — what happens to the product if one partner wants out? This protects both people.
  • A test before a commitment — co-host one webinar or co-create one free resource before building a full paid product together
