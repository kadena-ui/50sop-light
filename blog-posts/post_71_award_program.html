4
What makes an award
program worth winning
  • A rigorous selection process — the award is only as valuable as the credibility of the selection. Use judges, criteria, and transparency.
  • A clearly defined and valued recipient — the most powerful awards celebrate a specific type of person in a specific field. Specificity creates desire.
  • Winners who genuinely benefit — media coverage, speaking invitations, client referrals. The award must deliver real career value to the winner.
  • Consistency over cycles — an award that runs once is an event. An award that runs annually becomes an institution. Commit to the long game.
  • A community around the winners — alumni of the award form a self-reinforcing network that advocates for the programme and for each other
