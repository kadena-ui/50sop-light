Micro-SaaS explained
without the tech jargon
SaaS stands for Software as a Service.
Micro-SaaS is a small, focused version — built to solve one specific problem for one specific audience, delivered as a subscription.

It is not Salesforce.
It is not a platform with a hundred features.

It is a single tool that does one thing exceptionally well — and charges $19, $49, or $97 per month for access to it.

Built once. Updated occasionally. Subscribed to forever.
