How to pitch and
protect the relationship
  • Build the media kit first — audience size, demographics, engagement rates, and past placements. The kit makes the pitch professional and the conversation efficient.
  • Pitch the value alignment, not the reach — cause-aligned sponsors care more about who trusts you than how many follow you
  • Retain editorial control always — in writing. The sponsor funds the platform. They do not direct the content. Non-negotiable.
  • Start with brands already in your ecosystem — tools you use, events you attend, organisations you admire. The easiest sponsor to close is one who already knows you.
  • Price for annual renewal — one-off placements are transactions. Annual partnerships are relationships — and relationships compound.
