3
3 ways to structure
the licensing deal
Revenue Share
You get a % of every sale
They sell your curriculum to their audience. You receive 20–50% of every transaction with zero delivery effort
Flat License Fee
Annual or monthly access fee
They pay a set fee for the right to use your framework — regardless of how many times they deploy it
Seat-Based
Per-user or per-member pricing
They pay per person who accesses your content — scales automatically as their audience grows

What you need before
you can make the ask
  • A named methodology — unnamed expertise cannot be licensed or priced as a standalone asset
  • Documented outcomes — proof the framework works for the audience the partner serves
  • A one-page overview — what the curriculum covers, who it is for, what they leave with
  • A licensing rate card — your pricing for each deal structure, ready to share without negotiating from zero
  • A warm introduction — licensing deals almost always start with a relationship, not a cold email
