4
What makes a retreat
worth the premium
price tag
  • Sell the deliverable, not the days — price the retreat on the value of what participants walk away with, not the number of hours facilitated. A completed revenue strategy is worth $25,000 regardless of how long it took to build.
  • Pre-work is non-negotiable — the most transformative retreats begin before the first morning session. Structured pre-work assessments, individual interviews, and document reviews make the time together exponentially more productive.
  • Name the output on the sales page — "you will leave with a documented 12-month revenue strategy" is more compelling than "an immersive three-day experience." Name the deliverable in the title, the description, and the proposal.
  • Deliver a written document within 48 hours — the retreat summary, decisions made, agreements reached, and implementation plan delivered in writing is what separates a premium retreat from an expensive conversation.
  • Offer a 30-day check-in as standard — included in every retreat at no additional cost. It positions you as a partner in implementation, not just a facilitator — and it opens the door to the next engagement naturally.
