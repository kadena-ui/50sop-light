How to launch without
waiting for a
large audience
  • Launch with 10 to 20 founding members — a small, curated cohort generates the testimonials, culture, and energy that attracts the next wave. Do not wait for scale to begin.
  • Invite, do not announce — first members should be personally invited. The exclusivity of the invitation is the first signal of premium positioning.
  • Design the member experience before the platform — the platform is infrastructure. The experience is the product. Know what members feel and gain before choosing where it lives.
  • Measure retention, not just acquisition — a community retaining 90% at month 12 is worth 10x one churning 50% every 60 days. Retention is the only metric that matters.
  • Make the network the product — the most powerful retention mechanism is not your content. It is the introductions and relationships only available through continued membership.
