What a properly designed
retainer looks like
  • A defined scope — exactly what is included, and exactly what is not
  • A deliverable ceiling — monthly output that is fixed, not expandable on request
  • An annual rate review — built into the agreement before it begins
  • An out-of-scope process — when they ask for more, there is already a clear path to a separate engagement
