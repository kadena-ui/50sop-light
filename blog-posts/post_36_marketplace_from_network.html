2
5 steps to build your
marketplace from
your network
1
Identify the two sides of your market
Who are the buyers? Who are the sellers or providers they need? You sit between them.
2
Curate the supply side first
Recruit 10 to 20 vetted practitioners, vendors, or experts whose quality you can vouch for
3
Build the access mechanism
A directory, a referral system, or a matching platform — even a curated email service works at launch
4
Charge for listing or access
Providers pay to be featured. Buyers pay for vetted access. Transaction fees apply at scale.
5
Let the network effect take over
Every new participant makes the marketplace more valuable for everyone already in it

What makes your
marketplace worth
paying for
  • Curation is the product — anyone can Google a list. Your marketplace offers vetted, trusted, pre-qualified options.
  • Your reputation is the guarantee — buyers trust the marketplace because they trust you as its curator
  • The network compounds over time — each new provider and buyer makes the marketplace more valuable without additional effort from you
  • You control the standards — your criteria for inclusion become the quality signal that sets your marketplace apart
  • Exclusivity creates demand — a marketplace that not everyone can join is one that everyone wants to be in
