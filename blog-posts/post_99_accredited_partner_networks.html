What the network needs
before it can carry
your name
  • A methodology worth carrying — before building the network, the methodology must be proven, documented, and producing results that justify the credential. The network's value is tied entirely to your reputation.
  • Rigorous accreditation standards — who qualifies, what they must demonstrate, and what revokes the credential. The standards are the quality guarantee. Protect them as you would protect your name.
  • A directory that drives referrals to partners — the directory must actively direct client inquiries to your partners. If the directory produces no client flow, the network loses its value proposition within the first renewal cycle.
  • Legal IP protection before launch — your methodology name, your assessment tools, and your branded materials must be trademarked and protected before a single partner is accredited. The network is built on your IP.
  • An annual event that compounds the community — an annual partner summit creates belonging, elevates standards, introduces new content, and makes renewal feel like a privilege rather than an obligation
