How to upgrade your
workbook into a system
  • Add a sequence — tell them exactly what to do first, second, and third, not just what to reflect on
  • Define the outcome — what will they have completed by the end? Name it specifically.
  • Include decision points — a system helps buyers choose, not just think
  • Add accountability checkpoints — milestone markers that show progress and create momentum
  • Price it like a solution — systems start at $97, not $0
