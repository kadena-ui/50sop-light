3
How to build the
survey that sells
1
Choose one tension in your industry
The best surveys expose a gap between what practitioners believe and what is actually happening
2
Write 10 to 15 targeted questions
Mix quantitative (scale, percentage) and qualitative (open-ended) for publishable depth
3
Collect 100+ responses minimum
Use your email list, social following, and partner networks — offer a summary report as an incentive
4
Publish the findings with your analysis
Raw data is free. Your interpretation — what this means and what to do about it — is the product.
5
Pitch the data to media and associations
Three key stats + your interpretation = a media-ready pitch that most journalists will open
