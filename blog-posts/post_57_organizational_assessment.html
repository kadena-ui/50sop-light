How to position this
offer to reach the
decision-maker
  • Lead with risk, not culture — the C-suite and board respond to liability language. Frame the assessment as risk mitigation and the conversation changes immediately.
  • Go to General Counsel and CFO first — not HR. The people who control the budget for this work sit in legal and finance, not in people operations.
  • Quantify the cost of inaction — one employment lawsuit averages $200K to $500K in legal fees alone. Your assessment fee is the cheaper option — by far.
  • Offer a scoped diagnostic first — a lower-cost preliminary review that surfaces one or two issues creates urgency for the full engagement
  • Name your methodology — a named assessment framework positions this as a proprietary service, not a generic audit
