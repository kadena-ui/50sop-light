How to build and
launch in
30 days or less
  • Start with what you already give away — the frameworks, prompts, and tools you share in every engagement are the first vault entries. Document them before building anything new.
  • Launch with 10 curated assets — a vault with 10 high-quality, immediately usable tools is more compelling than one with 100 mediocre ones. Quality over volume, always.
  • Add one new asset per month minimum — the subscription is only justified if the vault grows. Monthly additions are the reason subscribers renew. Build the production cadence before you launch.
  • Sell to your existing clients first — your current and past clients already know the value of your thinking. They are the warmest possible subscribers and the strongest possible testimonials.
  • Offer team licences from day one — a single enterprise subscriber at $1,000 per month is equivalent to ten individual subscribers. Price and promote team access prominently from launch.
