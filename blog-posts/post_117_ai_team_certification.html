4
How to make your
certification the one
the market trusts
  • Build on a documented competency framework — the certification must assess specific, observable skills. Vague "AI awareness" programmes will not hold market credibility. Define exactly what certified practitioners can do that uncertified ones cannot.
  • Design for verifiability — a digital badge linked to a verifiable record is essential. Employers, procurement teams, and regulators need to confirm a certificate is genuine. Platforms like Credly make this straightforward.
  • Partner with an association or institution for endorsement — a trade association or professional body that endorses your certification adds third-party credibility that dramatically accelerates adoption among their membership.
  • Build the annual recertification from day one — AI is changing faster than any annual competency standard can fully capture. Recertification is both a quality commitment and a recurring revenue engine. Never issue a credential that does not expire.
  • Use AI to deliver the certification programme at scale — a custom GPT that guides learners through the curriculum, tests comprehension, and prepares them for assessment means you can certify hundreds of people without delivering every session yourself.
