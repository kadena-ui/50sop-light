4
The content that makes
the sales call unnecessary
  • Name the problem precisely — specific problem identification makes ideal clients feel you wrote it for them personally
  • Show the cost of inaction — what does staying stuck cost them in time, money, or momentum every month?
  • Demonstrate the methodology — teach a piece of your framework publicly so they experience your thinking before they pay for it
  • Make the offer visible — content that never mentions what you sell cannot convert what it attracts
  • Be consistent, not constant — one strategic piece per week outperforms daily posting with no strategic intent
