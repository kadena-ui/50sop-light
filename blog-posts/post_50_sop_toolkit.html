How to price and
package the
SOP toolkit
  • Name the toolkit — "The [Your Brand] Operating System" is a product. "My SOPs" is a folder. The name is the first signal of value.
  • Price by access tier — individual SOP bundles at $97 to $497, full toolkit license at $997 to $5,000, team license at $5,000 to $25,000
  • Include a setup guide — the SOP for implementing the SOPs. This reduces support burden and increases perceived value significantly.
  • Build in annual renewal — as your systems evolve, licensees pay annually to access updated versions — recurring revenue from a single asset
  • Pair it with onboarding — a 90-minute implementation session added to the toolkit doubles the price and quadruples the outcome
