How to build a dataset
that is worth licensing
  • Start collecting structured data intentionally from today — the most valuable datasets are built over time with consistent structure. Decide what you will track across every engagement and start capturing it in a format that can be aggregated and analysed.
  • Obtain consent at the point of collection — every engagement agreement should include a clause permitting anonymised use of data for research and product development. This consent is the legal foundation of any future licence.
  • Anonymise rigorously before any commercial use — individual client data must never be identifiable in a licensed dataset. Proper anonymisation is a legal requirement and a commercial necessity. Build it into your data management process from the start.
  • Add an annual survey to your existing audience — a structured annual survey of your clients, subscribers, or community generates fresh, original, licensable data every year. 500 consistent responses tracked over five years is a genuinely valuable longitudinal dataset.
  • Document the provenance and collection methodology — buyers of data for AI training need to know how the data was collected, when, from whom, and by what method. Documented provenance is what makes a dataset licensable rather than merely interesting.
