The course completion

4
Why bootcamps
are easier to
sell and scale
A course requires someone to imagine finishing it alone, at their own pace, with no deadline.
That is a hard sell.
A bootcamp requires someone to show up for 3 to 5 days and leave with a result.
That is an easy yes.
And because bootcamps run on a schedule, they create natural scarcity — limited spots, specific dates, a clear start and end.
Scarcity sells.
Community sells.
A deadline sells better than a discount every single time.
