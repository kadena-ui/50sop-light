The download myth that
keeps podcasters broke
You have been told that monetization comes after audience size.

It is not the only model.

Audience quality beats audience size.

What your podcast needs
before it can convert
  • A clear listener identity — who specifically is this show for, and what specific problem does it solve for them?
  • A consistent call to action — every episode ends with one clear next step, not five options
  • A visible offer — listeners cannot buy what they do not know exists. Mention it. Every time.
  • A repurposing system — each episode should feed at least three other content formats automatically
  • A premium tier — even 50 subscribers at $19/month is $950 in recurring revenue from your existing audience
