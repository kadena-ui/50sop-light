The anatomy of a
high-converting workshop
Open
Name the exact problem
They need to recognize themselves in the first 60 seconds — or they disengage
Build
Deliver a real partial result

What makes a workshop
sell without selling
  • Charge for it — even $27 creates commitment and filters for serious buyers. Free workshops attract browsers.
  • Deliver a real win — if they leave with something tangible, they trust you enough to buy the next step
  • Keep it under 90 minutes — the tighter the container, the higher the perceived value and completion rate
  • Record and resell it — a single live workshop becomes an evergreen product with one additional step
  • End with a bridge, not a pitch — show the logical next step, not a sales page. The difference is everything.
