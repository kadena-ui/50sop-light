4
How to fund and
sustain the open-access
library
  • Sponsor the library — foundations and corporations fund open-access educational resources as community investment. The library is a fundable asset.
  • Include sponsorship acknowledgement — visible recognition drives additional corporate interest in co-funding future resources
  • Gate with an email, not a payment — an email address for free resources builds your list without creating a payment barrier
  • Place a premium tier next to every free resource — the upgrade is always one click away. The library design is the sales funnel.
  • Update regularly and announce it — a growing library gives subscribers a reason to return — and a reason to upgrade
