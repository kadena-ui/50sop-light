The single-offer problem

What makes a bundle
feel like a gift, not a upsell
  • Every item solves a related problem — the bundle feels curated, not padded. Each addition earns its place.
  • The saving is visible and real — show the individual prices clearly so the discount feels genuine, not manufactured
  • The bundle has a name — "The Launch Kit" sells better than "3 items included" because it creates a product identity
  • It solves a complete journey — not just part of a problem, but the whole arc from where they are to where they want to be
  • It removes a decision — the bundle answers "what do I need?" before the buyer has to ask it
