What a digital product

What makes it a
marketplace, not just
a product page
  • A clear audience identity — the marketplace serves one specific type of buyer, not everyone. The niche is the brand.
  • Multiple products at multiple price points — a minimum of 5 to 8 products gives buyers a reason to browse and return
  • Products from more than one creator — even one partner product transforms a shop into a marketplace
  • A curation standard — not everything is included. Your quality filter is your reputation.
  • A reason to return — new drops, seasonal releases, or a subscription tier that rewards loyalty
