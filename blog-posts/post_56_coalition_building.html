4
How to price your
coalition building
correctly
  • Never price by meeting — coalition work is continuous. Retainer or project-based pricing captures the full scope of what you deliver.
  • Price for the outcome, not the process — what is a functional, aligned coalition worth to the funder? Price relative to that value, not your hours.
  • Separate phases into distinct contracts — mapping, building, and sustaining are three different engagements. Price each one accordingly.
  • Build in a sustainability fee — coalitions need ongoing facilitation. Negotiate the ongoing support contract at the start, not after the coalition is already dependent on you.
  • Document and publish your coalitions — case studies showing what coalitions you built and what they achieved are your most powerful new business tool
