Why CSR budgets are

The gap between what companies say they will do and what they actually deliver is enormous.
That gap is your consulting opportunity. Mission consultants who reframe their work as CSR delivery — with outcomes data, community relationships, and documented impact — access budgets that nonprofit consultants rarely reach.

How to position your
work inside CSR budgets
  • Lead with outcomes, not activities — CSR teams are measured on impact delivered, not events hosted. Position every offer around measurable results.
  • Target the CSR team directly — not marketing, not HR, not the CEO. The CSR team controls the budget and is actively looking for credible delivery partners.
  • Bring existing community relationships — the fastest path to a CSR contract is demonstrating that you already have the community access the company needs but lacks
  • Propose a pilot with a reporting component — a 90-day pilot with documented outcomes is the lowest-risk entry point for a company and the highest-credibility exit for you
  • Price for annual renewal — CSR programmes that deliver results are renewed. Build renewal into the original contract language from day one.
