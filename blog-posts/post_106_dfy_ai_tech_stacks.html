4
How to productise
this so it scales
beyond one client
  • Build an industry-specific starter stack — a pre-designed AI stack for law firms, or healthcare practices, or real estate teams is faster to deploy, easier to sell, and more credible than a generic "we'll figure it out" proposal
  • Document every build as a reusable template — the second implementation of any stack takes a fraction of the time. Your margin improves with every client while your price stays fixed.
  • Partner with AI tool vendors for referral fees — every tool you recommend and implement generates a potential affiliate or referral relationship. The stack build pays you. The tools pay you again.
  • Always include a maintenance retainer — AI tools update constantly. A client whose stack you built needs ongoing optimisation. Price that in from the start.
  • License the stack template to other consultants — once you have a proven AI stack for a specific industry, other consultants will pay to deploy your template under their own engagements
