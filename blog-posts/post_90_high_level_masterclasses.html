How to get booked
for the rooms that
matter most
  • Identify who runs the programmes you want to enter — every association has a VP of education, every corporate cohort has an L&D lead. These are your buyers, not the marketing team.
  • Pitch the advanced segment specifically — propose a masterclass designed explicitly for their most senior or most experienced members. Specificity commands premium fees and immediate attention.
  • Lead with outcomes, not topics — "After this masterclass, your senior leaders will leave with X, Y, and Z" outperforms any topic description. Decision-makers buy outcomes.
  • Include recording rights in the negotiation — the recording of a masterclass delivered live can be sold to members who missed it, creating a second revenue event from the same content
  • Propose the annual partner arrangement after the first session — a community whose members loved your masterclass will pay a premium to guarantee your availability for the next twelve months
