How to position yourself
as an advocacy
consultant
  • Name your niche clearly — "advocacy consultant" is broad. "Healthcare policy advocacy for community health organisations" is a practice that commands a premium.
  • Document your policy wins — legislation influenced, budgets shifted, policies changed. These are your case studies and they are worth more than any credential.
  • Price for outcomes, not hours — the value of a policy win cannot be measured in time. Scope your engagements around the change you will deliver.
  • Build your coalition credibility — the advocacy consultant with established relationships in the relevant policy ecosystem is worth multiples of one who is just starting
  • Publish your perspective — op-eds, briefings, and public commentary establish you as the voice on this issue before any client has a chance to hire you
