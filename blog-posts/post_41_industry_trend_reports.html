How to collect the
data without a
research team
  • Run an annual survey — 10 to 15 targeted questions sent to your audience and client base generates publishable data
  • Synthesize existing sources — public reports, industry publications, and your own client observations combined create original analysis
  • Interview 10 practitioners — structured conversations with peers yield quotable insights no desk research can replicate
  • Document your own client outcomes — year-over-year patterns from your own work are proprietary data no competitor has access to
  • Position your perspective as the analysis — readers do not pay for raw data. They pay for your interpretation of what it means.
