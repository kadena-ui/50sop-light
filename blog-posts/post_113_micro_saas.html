How to validate before
you build a single
line of code
  • Start with the problem you solve most often — the best Micro-SaaS idea is the solution you have built manually for clients over and over. The recurring problem is the product-market fit signal hiding in plain sight.
  • Validate with a manual version first — before building any software, deliver the outcome manually for 5 to 10 paying clients. If they pay for the result with a human doing the work, they will pay for software that does it faster.
  • Build with no-code tools first — Bubble, Glide, Softr, and AI-integrated tools like BuildAI allow a non-technical founder to build and launch a working product. Validate the market before investing in custom development.
  • Price for annual subscriptions from launch — annual pricing dramatically improves cash flow and reduces churn. A product with 100 annual subscribers at $599 has $59,900 in collected annual revenue before a single month-to-month subscriber joins.
  • Build the exit into the roadmap — a Micro-SaaS with documented recurring revenue, low churn, and a clear niche can be sold on platforms like Acquire.com for 3 to 5 times annual recurring revenue. Building to sell is a valid strategy from day one.
