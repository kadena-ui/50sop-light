4
Who to target and
how to close the
first deal
  • Target HR consulting firms first — they serve many clients, need consistent content, and have procurement budgets for training resources that save them build time
  • Approach leadership development companies — firms that design leadership programmes for corporate clients are perpetually in need of quality content to fill gaps in their current library
  • Lead with a sample module — give one complete, fully editable module as a demo. A buyer who sees the production quality and depth of your material closes faster than a buyer who only reads a description.
  • Package it professionally — a style guide, a branding replacement kit, and a facilitation note are what separate a licensable library from a folder of files. The packaging justifies the premium.
  • Protect your IP with a clear agreement — white-label is not a transfer of ownership. Your agreement must specify what can be branded, what cannot be modified, and what triggers licence revocation.
