How to capture insights
before they disappear
  • Keep a pattern journal — after every client engagement, note the recurring theme, the unexpected discovery, or the question that changed everything
  • Ask a debrief question — "What is the one thing you know now that you wish you had known at the start?" Their answer is your next product.
  • Document the decision behind the deliverable — not just what you produced, but why you made every major choice within it
  • Name the pattern when you see it repeating — the third time you observe the same dynamic across different clients is the moment a framework is born
  • Separate client IP from your IP — the specific output belongs to them; the thinking that produced it belongs to you
