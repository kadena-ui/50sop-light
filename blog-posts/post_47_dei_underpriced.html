4
How to reframe the
conversation to
command the right fee
  • Lead with business outcomes — retention cost, productivity data, innovation metrics. The business case is financial before it is cultural.
  • Sell to decision-makers, not gatekeepers — the executive who can write a $50K check is not in the same room as the person who books training
  • Name your methodology — a named framework positions DEI work as proprietary intellectual property, not a commodity service
  • Propose retainer, not hourly — ongoing equity work is relationship-based. Price the relationship, not the hours.
  • Document and publish outcomes — case studies that quantify the impact of your work make the next engagement easier to price at a premium
