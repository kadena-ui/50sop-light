What to negotiate
beyond the fee
  • Lead capture rights — a dedicated opt-in link or landing page promoted to the host's audience before, during, and after
  • Affiliate revenue share — a percentage of every sale generated from your appearance link or code
  • Co-promotion commitment — the host actively promotes the episode to their full email list, not just social
  • Content repurposing rights — full permission to use clips, transcripts, and the recording across your own channels
  • A back-end offer mention — a specific, agreed-upon CTA placed at a high-attention moment in the appearance
