4
What mission-driven
pricing actually
looks like
  • Full price for full capacity — clients who can pay full rate do. This is not greed. It is what funds everything else.
  • Sliding scale with a floor — you set the minimum that keeps the business viable. Below that floor, you do not go.
  • Scholarship applications, not discounts — access-reduced pricing is earned through a process, not assumed. This preserves value.
  • Mission surplus, not mission sacrifice — the goal is generating enough revenue to fund the impact — not operating at the edge of survival in service of it
  • Documented impact as a marketing asset — your mission outcomes are proof of methodology. Every impact story is a sales story.
