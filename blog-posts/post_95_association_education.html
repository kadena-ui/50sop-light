4
How to land your
first association
partnership
  • Start as a speaker, not a vendor — submit a session proposal for their annual conference. A successful session is the most efficient audition for an education partnership conversation that follows naturally.
  • Target the education director or VP of programmes — not the CEO, not the marketing team. The person who owns the member education budget is your decision-maker and your advocate.
  • Join the association as a member first — insider status, committee participation, and genuine community involvement create the relationships that formal proposals cannot.
  • Propose a pilot webinar at no cost — one complimentary member webinar that delivers visible, measurable value is worth more than any proposal document. Let the delivery make the case.
  • Negotiate exclusivity in your niche — the most valuable association partnerships include exclusivity clauses that prevent them from engaging a direct competitor in your specialty area for the duration of the agreement
