How to identify the book
only you can write
  • What do you say in every talk that people write down? — the insight that consistently stops rooms is the core of a book
  • What question does your industry keep getting wrong? — a contrarian position held with evidence and conviction is the most publishable thing you own
  • What do your clients need to understand before they can truly benefit from your work? — that prerequisite knowledge is the book
  • What is the story only you can tell? — lived experience, observed patterns, and documented outcomes no external researcher has access to
  • What would you regret not having written? — the book that feels most urgent is almost always the one that is most needed
