How to choose the
right platform and
avoid the pitfalls
  • Choose a platform with true white-label capability — your brand, your domain, your pricing. Platforms like GoHighLevel, Vendasta, or AI-native tools built for resellers offer genuine white-label infrastructure where your name is all the client ever sees.
  • Niche the positioning aggressively — "AI tools for coaches" is a market. "AI client management for executive coaches with teams under 10" is a product. The tighter the niche, the lower the competition and the higher the conversion rate.
  • Add your IP on top — templates, frameworks, and onboarding processes built from your expertise are what differentiate your branded tool from the raw platform. The platform is the infrastructure. Your methodology is the value.
  • Build a community around the tool — a private community for subscribers dramatically increases retention. Subscribers who are connected to other users stay 3 to 5 times longer than those using the tool in isolation.
  • Negotiate reseller terms carefully — understand the platform's pricing structure for resellers, the data portability terms, and what happens if the platform is acquired or discontinued. These risks need to be in your business model from day one.
