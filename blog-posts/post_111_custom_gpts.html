4
How to train and
protect your
intellectual property
  • Start with your existing content — every framework document, workshop guide, email sequence, and published article is training material. Audit and compile your content library before writing a single new prompt.
  • Name the GPT after your methodology, not the technology — a tool called "The Revenue Design Advisor" is a branded product. A tool called "My GPT" is a commodity. The name, the persona, and the visual identity are what make it licensable.
  • Build a subscription access model from day one — monthly access fees for your branded GPT create recurring revenue. A subscriber base of 200 people at $97 per month is $19,400 in monthly recurring revenue from one AI tool.
  • Document what the GPT cannot do — clear scope boundaries protect your liability and create natural upsell moments. When the AI reaches its limit, the human engagement begins.
  • Retrain quarterly at minimum — a GPT trained six months ago does not reflect your current thinking. Regular retraining is both a quality commitment and a reason to charge ongoing maintenance fees.
